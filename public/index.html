The Problem

Your booking system is selling your customers back to you. Every month.

A regular who books through Quandoo every Friday? You pay €2–€3 every single week for a customer who already knows and loves your restaurant. That's €100+ per year for one loyal guest. Multiply that across all your regulars.

200 bookings a month on Quandoo Premium? €99 + €400 in commission = €499/month. 500 bookings? €99 + €1,000 = €1,099/month. That's over €13,000 a year. You're being punished for being successful.

And the guest data — visit history, preferences, contact details? That lives on Quandoo's servers. Not yours. They use it to promote other restaurants — your competitors — to your own customers. You're building someone else's business with every booking.

Most of your Quandoo bookings aren't people browsing quandoo.fi looking for dinner. They're people who Googled your restaurant by name and happened to book through Quandoo's widget. You're paying commission on customers who were already coming to you.

Want a floor plan? Upgrade to Premium. Want a waitlist? Premium. Analytics? Premium. CRM tags to remember your regulars? Premium. Even basic business intelligence costs you €99/month before a single booking is made.
