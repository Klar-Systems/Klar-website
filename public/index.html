The Booking Fee Problem

Your booking system charges you every time a customer sits down.

A regular who books through Quandoo every Friday? You pay €2–4 per visit for a customer who already knows and loves your restaurant. That's up to €200 a year for one loyal guest. Multiply that across all your regulars.

And the guest data — visit history, preferences, contact details? That lives on Quandoo's servers, not yours. They use it to promote other restaurants to your own customers. You're building someone else's business with every booking.
